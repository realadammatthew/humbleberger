Our Mission

Humbleberger Ministries exists to glorify God by proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ, with a focused calling to bring the good news to the Jewish people in cities like New York and Tel Aviv.

Rooted in the spirit of the Apostle Paul, our mission is to evangelize, disciple, and raise up faithful preachers and teachers to plant and shepherd churches across the globe.

While our primary focus is Jewish missions, we stand fully on the Great Commission, seeking to reach all the lost and serve the global Church as the Lord leads.
